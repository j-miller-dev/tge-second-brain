# Second Brain Home

This vault now follows a layered note system for capture, reading, and synthesis.

## Main areas
- [[Inbox/Welcome]] for quick capture and raw ideas
- [[Literature Notes/Index]] for lecture notes, book notes, and source-based summaries
- [[Permanent Notes/Index]] for atomic ideas and standalone insights
- [[Projects/Index]] for active plans, checklists, and trackers
- [[Work/Index]] for work notes and decisions
- [[Kindle/Index]] for reading notes and highlights
- [[Discrete Math/Index]] for domain-specific study organization

## Note-taking rules
- Use the Inbox for fleeting notes; review and move them regularly.
- Keep literature notes tied to a source and written in your own words.
- Make permanent notes atomic: one idea per note, self-contained, and densely linked.
- Keep projects separate from knowledge notes.
