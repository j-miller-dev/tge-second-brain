# Permanent Notes

Use this area for atomic, standalone ideas that can survive on their own.

## Rules
- One idea per note.
- Keep it short and self-contained.
- Link it densely to other notes.
- Prefer concept names over source names.

## Suggested use
- Extract a single insight from a literature note.
- Link it to related concepts across domains.
